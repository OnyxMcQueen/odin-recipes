Pizza

Description

Our Classic Pizza features a perfectly crispy crust topped with a rich tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and your choice of favorite toppings. The dough is made from scratch, allowing it to rise and develop a delightful texture that’s both chewy and crisp. After spreading a layer of tangy tomato sauce, it’s generously covered with mozzarella cheese, which melts into a deliciously gooey layer.

Baked to perfection in a hot oven, this pizza comes out with a golden crust and bubbling cheese. Whether you choose to add pepperoni, mushrooms, or bell peppers, each slice delivers a satisfying combination of flavors that will make this classic pizza a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • For the dough
    • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
    • 1 1/2 cups warm water
    • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1 teaspoon salt
  • For the sauce
    • 1 cup tomato sauce
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the topping
    • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional garnishes
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Olive oil for drizzling
