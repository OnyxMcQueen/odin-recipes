Pizza

Description

Our Classic Pizza features a perfectly crispy crust topped with a rich tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and your choice of favorite toppings. The dough is made from scratch, allowing it to rise and develop a delightful texture that’s both chewy and crisp. After spreading a layer of tangy tomato sauce, it’s generously covered with mozzarella cheese, which melts into a deliciously gooey layer.

Baked to perfection in a hot oven, this pizza comes out with a golden crust and bubbling cheese. Whether you choose to add pepperoni, mushrooms, or bell peppers, each slice delivers a satisfying combination of flavors that will make this classic pizza a staple in your recipe collection.

Ingredients

  • For the dough
    • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
    • 1 1/2 cups warm water
    • 3 1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1 teaspoon salt
  • For the sauce
    • 1 cup tomato sauce
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the topping
    • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Optional garnishes
    • Fresh basil leaves
    • Olive oil for drizzling

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough
    1. In a small bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for about 5 minutes, or until it becomes frothy.
    2. In a large bowl, combine 3 1/2 cups of flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
    3. Mix until the dough begins to come together, then turn it out onto a floured surface and knead for about 5-7 minutes, adding more flour as needed, until the dough is smooth and elastic.
    4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a damp cloth or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-2 hours, or until it has doubled in size.
  2. Prepare the sauce
    1. In a small bowl, combine the tomato sauce, dried oregano, dried basil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix well and set aside.
  3. Preheat the oven
    1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats.
  4. Shape the dough
    1. Punch down the risen dough and divide it into two portions (for two pizzas) or keep it whole for one large pizza.
    2. On a floured surface, roll out the dough to your desired thickness and shape. If you’re using a pizza stone, transfer the rolled-out dough onto a piece of parchment paper.
  5. Assemble the pizza
    1. Spread a thin layer of the prepared tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
    2. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce. Add any additional toppings you like.
  6. Bake the pizza
    1. Transfer the pizza (with parchment paper, if using a stone) onto a baking sheet or pizza stone.
    2. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Finish and Serve
    1. Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes.
    2. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and a drizzle of olive oil, if desired.
    3. Slice and enjoy your homemade classic pizza!
