Tacos

Description

Tacos are a beloved Mexican dish made up of soft or crispy tortillas filled with a variety of flavorful ingredients. Traditionally, they feature seasoned meat like beef, chicken, or pork, but can also be made with fish, beans, or vegetables. Toppings such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, cheese, salsa, and a squeeze of lime add layers of freshness and flavor.

Each taco offers a perfect balance of savory fillings, fresh toppings, and the signature texture of the tortilla. Whether enjoyed as a quick snack or a full meal, tacos are versatile and customizable, making them a favorite for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • For the taco filling
    • 1 pound (450 grams) ground beef, chicken, or pork
    • 1 packet taco seasoning mix (or homemade taco seasoning)
    • 1/2 cup water
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil (for cooking)
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • For the toppings
    • Shredded lettuce
    • Diced tomatoes
    • Shredded cheese (cheddar, Monterey Jack, or your choice)
    • Sour cream
    • Salsa or taco sauce
    • Diced onions
    • Sliced jalapeños (optional)
    • Fresh cilantro (optional)
    • Lime wedges (for squeezing over tacos)
  • For the tortillas
    • 8-10 small soft flour or corn tortillas (or crispy taco shells)

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
    1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
    2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until soft and fragrant (about 2-3 minutes).
    3. Add the ground beef (or chicken/pork) to the skillet, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Cook until browned and fully cooked through (about 6-8 minutes).
  2. Add the taco seasoning
    1. Once the meat is browned, drain any excess fat.
    2. Stir in the taco seasoning mix and add 1/2 cup of water. Mix well to combine.
    3. Simmer the mixture for 3-4 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken.
  3. Prepare the tortillas
    1. While the meat is cooking, warm the tortillas. You can heat them in a dry skillet over medium heat for about 30 seconds on each side or warm them in the microwave by wrapping them in a damp paper towel and heating for about 20-30 seconds.
  4. Assemble the tacos
    1. Spoon the seasoned meat mixture into the center of each tortilla or taco shell.
    2. Add your desired toppings such as shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, and any other favorite ingredients.
  5. Serve
    1. Serve the tacos immediately with a squeeze of lime over the top for added flavor.
