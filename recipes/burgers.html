Chicken Burgers

Description

The Classic Chicken Sandwich features a perfectly seasoned, juicy chicken breast that’s breaded and fried to a golden crisp. Nestled between a soft, toasted bun, the chicken is complemented by crisp lettuce, ripe tomato slices, and a dollop of creamy mayonnaise. The result is a satisfying, savory sandwich with a delightful crunch and fresh, flavorful toppings.

To elevate this classic, a high-quality chicken breast is marinated to enhance its flavor and tenderness. Each bite offers a combination of crispy coating and tender meat, making it a hearty and enjoyable meal. Whether for lunch or dinner, this chicken sandwich stands as a timeless favorite, sure to please anyone who enjoys a well-crafted sandwich.

Ingredients

  • For the chicken patties
    • 1 pound (450 grams) ground chicken
    • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
    • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
    • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
    • 1 clove garlic, minced
    • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (or 1 teaspoon dried parsley)
    • 1 teaspoon paprika
    • 1/2 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1 egg (to help bind the ingredients)
  • For the burger assembly
    • 4 hamburger buns
    • Lettuce leaves
    • Tomato slices
    • Pickles (optional)
    • 4 slices of cheese (optional, e.g., cheddar or Swiss)
    • Mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, or your preferred condiments
  • For cooking
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil or vegetable oil (for frying or grilling)

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken patties
    1. In a large bowl, combine ground chicken, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese (optional), chopped onion, minced garlic, parsley, paprika, salt, pepper, and the egg. Mix until well combined.
    2. Form the mixture into equal-sized patties, about 4 in total.
  2. Cook the chicken patties
    1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
    2. Place the chicken patties in the pan and cook for about 4-5 minutes on each side, or until the internal temperature reaches 165°F (75°C) and the patties are golden brown and fully cooked.
  3. Toast the buns
    1. While the patties cook, lightly toast the hamburger buns on a skillet or in a toaster.
  4. Assemble the sandwiches
    1. Spread your preferred condiments (mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard, etc.) on the toasted buns.
    2. Add lettuce, tomato slices, and pickles to the bottom half of the bun.
    3. Place the cooked chicken patty on top of the toppings, and add a slice of cheese if desired.
  5. Serve
    1. Place the top bun on the sandwich, and serve immediately while warm.
